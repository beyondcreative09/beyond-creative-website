How pricing works

Our retainers are based on the number of channels, complexity of your funnel, and the speed at which you want to move.

  • 3-month minimum to give experiments time to work.
  • Monthly retainers – no lock-in after the initial period.
  • Ad spend is billed directly on your platforms, not through us.
